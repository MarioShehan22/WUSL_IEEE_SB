Career Compass – Career Guidance Session 2025

Guiding school students across the North Western Province

Event Gallery

About This Event

Helping students discover strengths and plan their futures

The Career Compass session held at Wayamba University provided valuable guidance to school students across the North Western Province. Designed to help students discover their strengths and plan their future careers, the session covered insights into higher education, career planning, and personal development.

The interactive engagement covered:

  • Higher education pathways and skills mapping
  • Career planning frameworks students can apply
  • Personal development and confidence-building
  • Q&A with practical next steps

With inspiring inputs and active participation from students, the event was a meaningful step toward shaping the next generation of Sri Lankan professionals.
