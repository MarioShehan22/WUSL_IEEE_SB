About This Event

Celebrating the strength and spirit of women at WUSL

To mark International Women’s Day 2025, the IEEE WIE Affinity Group of WUSL hosted SHe Too 4.0 — a dynamic Game Zone packed with excitement, teamwork, and empowerment.

The event brought together enthusiastic participants for a day of interactive games, laughter, and celebration, creating a joyful space that honored unity, inclusion, and community spirit.

  • Interactive team games and challenges
  • Inclusive activities fostering collaboration
  • Positive, supportive atmosphere for everyone

Organized by: IEEE WIE Student Branch Affinity Group – Wayamba University of Sri Lanka.
