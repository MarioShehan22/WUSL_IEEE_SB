About This Event

A Step Beyond the Hype: A Journey Through Modern AI

This insightful online session explored how AI is shaping industries and careers globally. With over 60 participants, the talk covered AI fundamentals, real-world applications, emerging trends, and career paths.

The interactive Q&A session addressed key topics like:

  • Project building strategies
  • Ethics in AI development
  • Future opportunities in the field
  • Career pathways in machine learning

This engaging event was designed specifically for undergraduates and tech enthusiasts looking to understand the practical applications of AI in today's world.
