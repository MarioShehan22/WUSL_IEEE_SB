About This Event

Celebrating problem solving, precision, and teamwork

WayaXtreme 3.0 brought together passionate coders for an intense 12-hour programming challenge designed to test skills, speed, and strategy. The event witnessed outstanding participation and remarkable problem-solving abilities from students across regions.

The competition was conducted in collaboration with:

  • IEEE SB CET (Kerala Section)
  • IEEE SB LBSITW (Kerala Section)
  • IEEE SB UPBM (Colombian Caribbean Section)

Congratulations to all the winners and participants who demonstrated technical excellence and innovation! Special kudos to the champions Team ALGOMINDS, and runners-up Team KEY and Team WAYAMITES.
