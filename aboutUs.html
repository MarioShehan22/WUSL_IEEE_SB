Our Mission

The IEEE Computer Society is dedicated to advancing the theory, practice, and application of computer science and engineering. It promotes innovation, education, and the exchange of ideas in computing and IT fields.

Professional Standards

We promote high professional standards and support collaboration between academia and industry in computing disciplines, under the global IEEE network.
