Welcome to IEEE Student Branch

The IEEE Student Branch at Wayamba University of Sri Lanka is dedicated to fostering technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. We provide students with opportunities to connect, learn, and grow within the global IEEE community.

Our Mission

Our Student Branch promotes various fields including Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Electronics, and Communications. We organize technical workshops, seminars, competitions, and professional development programs to enhance our members' academic and career prospects.

Our Journey

Branch Establishment
January 2023

IEEE Student Branch was officially established at Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, marking the beginning of our journey in advancing engineering and technology education.

May 2023

Successfully conducted our first major technical workshop "Byte & Build" - an online game development workshop that attracted participants from across the university.
