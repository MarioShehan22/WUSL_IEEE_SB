Spectrum of Light 2025 – Vesak Photography Competition

Celebrating Vesak through natural photography and AI-generated imagery

Event Gallery

About This Event

Uniting tradition and technology through creativity

Organized by the IEEE Student Branch of Wayamba University of Sri Lanka, in collaboration with the IEEE WIE Affinity Group and Computer Society Chapter, “Spectrum of Light 2025” celebrated Vesak with a creative photography competition across two categories: Natural Photography and AI-Generated Images.

Participants captured the spirit of light, peace, and reflection. Winners were selected by a judging panel and through Facebook popularity.

  • Best Photograph: T.D. Amaranayake (NSBM)
  • Most Popular: H.B.M.H.S. Mahagedara (WUSL)
  • Best AI Photo: W.M.H. Piyumika (WUSL)
  • Most Popular AI: D.A.D.A. Daranagama (WUSL)

The event brought together tradition and innovation—promoting creativity, cultural appreciation, and community engagement.
