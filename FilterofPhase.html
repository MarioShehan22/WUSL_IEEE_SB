Filter of Hope – Phase 5

Deep Well Solar Water Pump for rural communities in Kuliyapitiya

Event Gallery

About This Event

Clean, sustainable water for communities that need it most

Filter of Hope – Phase 5 marked another impactful step toward providing clean and sustainable water access to rural communities. This phase introduced a Deep Well Solar Water Pump, enabling long-term improvements across health, education, and daily life in Kuliyapitiya.

The event was filled with meaningful engagement, compassion, and community spirit—reflecting IEEE’s mission for humanitarian impact.

  • Renewable, reliable water supply
  • Sustainable design with low operating cost
  • Direct benefits to students and local families
  • Community collaboration and stewardship
